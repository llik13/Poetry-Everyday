About this poem

"–ü–∞—Ä—É—Å" (The Sail) is one of Lermontov's most famous poems, written in 1832 when the poet was only 18 years old. This poem reflects the romantic spirit of the era and captures the essence of a restless soul seeking meaning beyond contentment.
