Frequently Asked Questions

Your feedback is reviewed by our team. For bugs and technical issues, we prioritize them based on severity. For feature suggestions, we evaluate them against our roadmap. If you provided an email address and granted permission, we may contact you for additional information.

If you provided contact information, we'll update you when there are significant developments. You can also check our "Platform Updates" section in the forum, where we post information about recent fixes and improvements based on user feedback.

We prioritize feature requests based on several factors: how many users have requested similar features, alignment with our platform's goals, technical feasibility, and potential benefit to the community. While we can't implement every suggestion, we value all feedback and use it to shape our development roadmap.

Yes, providing your name and email address is optional. However, without contact information, we won't be able to follow up with you about your feedback or let you know when issues are resolved or features are implemented.

In addition to this feedback form, you can participate in discussions on our forum's "Site Suggestions" board, join our monthly community calls, or reach out directly to our support team. We also occasionally run surveys and focus groups when planning major updates to the platform.
